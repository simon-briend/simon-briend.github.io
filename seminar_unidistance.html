About the Seminar

[The PSYR seminar] (probability and statistics young researchers seminar) is a bi-weekly meeting. The goal is to share and discover the work of young colleagues in an informal setting. We strongly encourage speakers to present accessible talks, followed by discussions and exchange. Talks are 40 minutes long, followed by questions and discussions. The aim of this seminar is to share results of young researchers, and we welcome all contributions in probability, statistics and beyond.

[When] We meet once every two weeks on Wednesday at 10am. The next talk is announced below.

[Where] Usually online. You can attend via this Zoom link.
